Brief description

We have worked with a few children to ensure that the courses that we give are not only entertaining, but also educational. We aim to bring fun into the classroom while kids grow a passion for our subjects. We offer classes to kindergarteners and other young kids in a variety of different subjects. We offer mainly math courses, but we also touch on writing and other subjects like science and history.
